The Planning Phase

Story Template: The 3 Question Framework

Write a comprehensive prompt that I can use to build this app…
  1. 01 — What problem am I trying to solve?

    I want to build an app that [describe the problem you're solving in plain English].

  2. 02 — Who is this for?

    It's for [specific person or group] who struggle with [their pain point].

  3. 03 — How should the solution feel?

    The app should feel [emotional quality] and look [visual style].

Example Prompt 01 · Budget Dashboard Write a comprehensive prompt that I can use to build this app…
  1. 01

    I want to build a [budget variance dashboard app to better manage my company's spend. It should have a table with these columns: Category, Planned Budget, Actual Spend, Variance (dollar amount), and Variance (percentage). For now, pre-populate it with 6 sample rows using realistic finance categories like Payroll, Marketing, Software Licenses, Travel, Office Supplies, and Consulting Fees. Make the variance cells green when under budget and red when over budget.]

  2. 02

    It's for [my finance department] who struggle with [having a centralized visual display of variance].

  3. 03

    The app should feel [simple, easy to read] and look [clean and minimalistic].

Example Prompt 02 · OKR / KPI Tracker Write a comprehensive prompt that I can use to build this app…
  1. 01

    I want to build an app that [helps me track my OKRs and KPIs for the year, as I do many tasks throughout the week, but there's no way for me to know if the tasks are aligning with my KPIs. I don't know what the gaps are and what percentage I've completed].

  2. 02

    It's for [me and my colleagues] who struggle with [keeping track of their KPIs].

  3. 03

    The app should feel [simple, easy to read] and look [clean and minimalistic].
